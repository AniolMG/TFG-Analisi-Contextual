Behind the scene... On a chilly Saturday night I came home, took a cold beer and sat down to rest a bit. I turned on the radio and one of the best songs that were ever written was playing. I wanted more. I know. There are tons of music services out there (believe me, I tried them all) but what I really wanted is something simple that looks good and gives me the music I love for free. The next day I called my friend and we started building StreamSquid, a place where you can discover, create and listen to the music you love. No registration required, No monthly fees, No complicated UI and functions. Just Music. The music you love. And hey, if it's good enough for us, you should enjoy it too. StreamSquid is now open to the world. If you enjoy it as much as we do, spread the word and let your friends know about it too. Thanks!
