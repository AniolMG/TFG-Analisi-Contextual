Men's Wallets
Is your wallet bursting at the seams? Can’t find that credit card as quickly as you’d hoped? It might be time to shop men’s wallets, available at Macy’s. A sleek wallet is part of your image, helps define your style and protects your personal items. Keep those essentials handy when you head out with a wallet for men designed to keep you organized. Check out our top men’s wallet styles now.
Bifold Wallet
A simple bifold wallet is always a timeless option. This style of men’s wallet features two sections that fold in half to conveniently slip into your back pocket. Often designed in supple leather, a bifold wallet is a convenient, fashionable pick that lasts. Do you need to produce your identification quickly? Get your hands on a passcase wallet, a bifold style designed with an extra slot for your ID.
Trifold Wallet
The roomier version of the bifold, trifold wallets for men work for any occasion. This style features more slots and spaces for cards, coins and bills. Often chunkier and bulkier than its counterpart, a trifold wallet is functional and exceptionally durable.
RFID Wallet
Are you worried about protecting your personal data? An RFID wallet may be a great choice if you carry cards that use RFID technology (like tap-and-go credit cards). This style of men’s wallet works by using built-in carbon fiber or metal material to block the electromagnetic field around your cards.
Card Case Wallet
Are you on the hunt for men’s accessories that are simple in design but big on convenience? Look no further than a card case wallet. This type of men’s wallet is an ultra-slim option designed to carry just a few cards—no fuss, no bulk. Card case wallets can slide effortlessly into any pocket for ultimate convenience.
Front Pocket Wallet
If minimalism is your style, you’ll love a front pocket wallet. No more sitting on that bulky wallet in your back pocket anymore, this style of men’s wallet offers a slim profile with room for a few cards and some folded bills.
Money Clips
Convenience without any bulk, money clips are a clever way to carry cash. With a super slim profile, you can slip your money clip into any pocket.
